About Us

At Oriental Detective, we pride ourselves on being more than just an investigative agency – we are your dedicated partner in uncovering the truth, ensuring your peace of mind, and safeguarding your interests. Our team consists of seasoned professionals with years of experience in the investigative field, offering a broad spectrum of services tailored to meet the distinct needs of individuals, businesses, and legal entities. We specialize in personal investigations, including discreet infidelity inquiries, comprehensive background checks, and efforts to locate missing persons, providing sensitive and compassionate support during challenging times. For businesses, we offer corporate solutions to detect and prevent employee fraud, safeguard intellectual property, and resolve workplace harassment issues, all while ensuring that your organization's reputation and integrity are protected. Additionally, our legal investigative services are designed to provide critical evidence for litigation support, perform due diligence for high-stakes transactions, and uncover hidden details that can make a crucial difference in the outcome of a case.....
